href="https://iowaclimate.org/2022/06/30/just-throw-money-aemo-demands-12b-gift-to-build-10000-km-of-new-transmission-lines-for-renewables/" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date published" href="https://iowaclimate.org/2022/06/30/just-throw-money-aemo-demands-12b-gift-to-build-10000-km-of-new-transmission-lines-for-renewables/" rel="permalink"><h1 class="entry-title">Just throw money: AEMO demands $12b gift to build 10,000 km of new transmission lines for&nbsp;Renewables</h1></a>\n\t\t</div><!-- .entry-header-column -->\n\t</div><!-- .entry-header-row -->\n</header><!-- .entry-header -->\n\t<div class="entry-content">\n\t<div>\n<div>\n<div>\n<p><span>E</span>ffectively &#8212; the AEMO (the Australian Energy Market Operator) is the taxpayer funded advertising agency for the Renewables Industry. The point of the latest AEMO super-report, apparently, is to get Australian taxpayers or consumers to foot the bill for the high voltage lines that the unreliable industry desperately needs but can&#8217;t pay for itself.</p>\n<p>The AEMO has declared we need to rush to cough up $12.7 billion to build new interconnectors in Australia. That&#8217;s $500 from every man woman and child and let&#8217;s call it what it is, a Gift Card for the Renewables Industry. The net benefit of all that money will be to allow wind and solar industrial plants to connect their unreliable product to the grid we already have, and to the storage products that we still have to pay for, and all so that their green electrons will make the weather 0.0 degrees cooler in a hundred years. Australians alive today will pay now and basically get nothing but views of more criss-crossy-steel-wires and spires, and more wind towers too. &#8220;Sing Hallalujah&#8221;. <em><br /></em></p>\n<p>The 104 page <em>Blueprint</em> imagines all kinds of scenarios except for an actual free market, true competition, consumer choice, or whether it makes any sense to use our national grid as a global climatic weather controller. It&#8217;s a fantasy document which includes four flavours of future electrical icecream: &#8220;Slow&#8221;, &#8220;Progressive&#8221;, &#8220;Step&#8221; or &#8220;Hydrogen Superpower&#8221;. Will that be a double-scoop with Spiderman topping? Yes indeedy &#8212; and with $28 billion dollars of imaginary savings to go. The word <em>blackout</em> appears no times. Nor is there a scenario for a &#8220;Cost effective&#8221; electrical grid. (Remember them?)</p>\n<p>These people are either crazy or paid far too much.The Hydrogen Superpower fixation comes straight out of Marvel comic. Wait til you see the graph.</p>\n<p>It&#8217;s rare to see this much abject nonsense in a single story.&#160; Perry Williams at <em>The Australian</em> just took the junk prospectus and ran it:</p>\n<p>Australia must accelerate a move away from coal to renewables and storage and urgently sanction more than $10bn of transmission projects to escape the ongoing threat of blackouts and high power prices amid a national &#173;energy crisis.</p>\n<p>Follow the logic: if having a bit less coal <em>causes</em> a crisis, then how exactly, will having <em>no coal</em> solve it?</p>\n<p>The Australian Energy Market Operator, which runs the &#173;national electricity network, said the country was undergoing a &#8220;complex, rapid and <strong>irreversible&#8221;</strong> change to its energy system&#8230;</p>\n<p>Is this the same &#8220;irreversible change&#8221; that is currently reversing all over Europe? The one where Germany, France, the UK, Austria and Poland are all using more coal?</p>\n<p>And isn&#8217;t the follow up just a tiny scary?</p>\n<p>an irreversible<strong>&#8221;</strong> &#8230;&#160; that would need a<strong> nine-fold increase in wind and solar capacity by 2050 to meet the nation&#8217;s net-zero emissions targets.</strong></p>\n<p>What kind of &#8220;rapid&#8221; irreversible transition is only 11% of the way out of the starting gate, but already falling over, costing a bomb, and we have to do this nine times more?</p>\n<h4>But wait. Renewables will not only make floods nicer, and fish happier, it make us safer from enemies?</h4>\n<p>&#8220;One source of energy that no geopolitical situation can interrupt in relation to our supply chains and that&#8217;s the sun to our land-mass and the wind on and off our shores. That&#8217;s good energy security and storing that is a matter of national security,&#8221; Mr Bowen told the National Press Club.</p>\n<p>What if the enemy just attacks at night and when there is no wind?</p>\n<p>Probably they won&#8217;t have to attack at all. As we go broke, they can just buy the nation out from under us.</p>\n<h4>There they go again: The AEMO calls coal and gas <em>volatile</em>, and renewables <em>cheap</em></h4>\n<p>The energy supply crunch that forced&#160;<a href="https://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/mining-energy/australian-energy-market-operator-says-electricity-spot-market-across-the-eastern-states-suspended/news-story/7d13e60e8162aa872657da2c922c1133" target="_self" rel="noopener">the suspension of the country&#8217;s power market for the first time</a>&#160;this month underscored the need for the Australian electricity grid to curb its exposure to the volatile commodities of coal and gas and fast-track cheap renewables backed up by storage, the AEMO said.</p>\n<p>The price of coal and gas isn&#8217;t volatile, it&#8217;s not going up and down, it&#8217;s gone up and stayed up &#8212;&#160; because it&#8217;s so essential, everyone has to have it and they want more than they can get. And the true price of renewables isn&#8217;t cheap, it&#8217;s just hidden &#8212; like this giant report pretending to hide that twelve billion dollars worth of interconnectors are entirely frivolous parts of a grid powered by centralized reliable power. We don&#8217;t need them. The Renewables Industry does.</p>\n<p id="vidora-top"><strong>It&#8217;s like a grab-bag of energy-platitudes</strong></p>\n<p>&#8220;I think recent events in Australia and overseas have really just underscored the need for &#173;urgent investment in renewables, firming and transmission so that we can de-link ourselves <strong>from these international factors</strong> and provide Australian homes and businesses with the most affordable, secure and reliable energy,&#8221; AEMO chief executive Daniel Westerman told The Australian.</p>\n<p>Since when was coal and gas an &#8220;international factor&#8221; for a nation which is the worlds largest exporter of coal and <a href="https://www.worldstopexports.com/petroleum-gas-exports-country/">third largest for gas</a>?</p>\n<h3>The costs of these renewable-interconnectors are staggering</h3>\n<p>Just throw money:</p>\n<p><a href="https://joannenova.com.au/wp-content/more-interconnectors-hidden-renewables-cost.jpg"><img src="https://joannenova.com.au/wp-content/more-interconnectors-hidden-renewables-cost-m1.jpg" alt="AEMO\'s Transmission Wishlist"></a></p>\n<p>The Marinus Link across the Bass Strait will cost at least <a href="https://www.examiner.com.au/story/7801521/barnett-pushing-fair-deal-on-marinus-link-as-questions-intensify/">$3.8 billion</a> and generate zero watts of electricity. Theoretically it will allow 1.5 GW of electricity to&#160; slip through, but for the same price, we could build a whole new advanced coal plant instead. Various iterations of governments here have already spent $200 million just on the feasibility study. Ten years ago they could nearly build a whole gas plant for that, one that wouldn&#8217;t be essentially useless for five months in a row if a shark chewed on it.</p>\n<p>The <a href="https://infrastructurepipeline.org/project/victoria-to-nsw-interconnector-west#:~:text=AEMO\'s%202020%20ISP%20estimates%20that,works%20up%20to%20%24150%20million.">VNI link</a> will cost $2 &#8211; $3 billion and make it possible for&#160; groups of renewable investors to make profits they otherwise couldn&#8217;t have.&#160; <a href="https://www.vu.edu.au/about-vu/news-events/news/humelink-to-increase-nsw-power-bills-cost-the-economy">Humelink</a> will be some kind of nightmare of $3b &#8211; $5 billion.</p>\n<h3>The unreliables are dead without these new transmission lines:</h3>\n<p>To put it in perspective a very annoyed high honcho at Snowy Hydro was not happy with the draft version of this document a few months ago and complained that the AEMO wasn&#8217;t pushing hard enough for faster &#8220;investment&#8221; in the VNI line which, ahem, just happens to be nearer to the Snowy Scheme, and which they wanted a lot more than the link to their competitors in Tasmania.&#160; In that complaint he quietly gave away that these transmission lines are essential in a life and death kind of way for renewables.</p>\n<p>[Snowy Hydro] has previously warned the lack of transmission <a href="https://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/snowy-hydro-wants-transmission-fasttracked/news-story/f8064f7abadf2fe5ae43612cdccc404f">could kill the transition to &#173;renewables</a>&#160;&#8211; with a string of major players weighing into the debate &#8211; and singled out concerns over infrastructure as a major issue that needs to be confronted to ensure supplies can flow to users.</p>\n<p>Renewable developers and&#160;<a href="https://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/energy-players-warn-of-grid-gap-in-trend-towards-renewables/news-story/5ac2f83c28f7a7537f4cb4b52c2c80dd" target="_self" rel="noopener">network operators are worried</a>&#160;a pipeline of power generation and clean energy supplies faces delays or gridlock unless major electricity transmission projects are delivered across the national power system.</p>\n<p><a href="https://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/mining-energy/snowy-hydro-warns-of-high-costs-and-potential-blackouts-if-aemos-energy-blueprint-proceeds/news-story/7001143dc33a35157c0b4d81c76e0415">&#8211;The Australian Feb 9th 2022</a></p>\n<p>REFERENCE</p>\n<p>AEMO &#160;<a height="864"></p>\n<p>Everyone can see that a kind of Star Chamber hides in Biden\xe2\x80\x99s shadow and dictates what he says and signs. Over the last year and a half, the game plan has been revealed by the exercise of authority assumed by federal and state Democratic Socialistic party.\xc2\xa0 The platform below summarized what we have witnessed from these political operatives.</p>\n<p><a href="https://rclutz.files.wordpress.com/2019/02/diversity.png"><img src="https://rclutz.files.wordpress.com/2019/02/diversity.png?w=1000" alt="" class="wp-image-9514"></a></p>\n<h5><strong>Establish Wokeism as the new state religion.</strong></h5>\n<p>Under the guise of \xe2\x80\x9cDiversity-Inclusion-Equity, the doctrine is to eliminate pluralism from American Life. Critical Race and Gender Theories, among others, are to be asserted as secular truths, and all other beliefs are to be banned from the public square in the name of separation of church and state.</p>\n<p>In the cult of Woke, adherents have a \xe2\x80\x9ccritical consciousness\xe2\x80\x9d and are able to see the \xe2\x80\x9cproblematics\xe2\x80\x9d in everything. This includes in speech, writing, institutions, thoughts, people, systems, knowledge, history, one\xe2\x80\x99s past, and society itself. Society is broken into different groups or classes (social group identity) that are oppressive on one side, oppressed on the other, and in conflict over this. That is, conflict theory is the belief that different social groups in society are always in conflict with one another for power and dominance.\xc2\xa0 And rather than working together in complex, dynamical ways that can be mutually beneficial, they are at war.</p>\n<p>Any criticism or questioning of Woke Doctrine is: racist, homophobic, transphobic, xenophobic, imperialistic, hateful, bigoted, unjust, evil, ignorant, wrong, and a crime against humanity.</p>\n<p><img width="735" src="https://mediacloud.theweek.com/image/private/s--Syau1Bel--/f_auto,t_single-media-image-desktop@1/v1608644184/cb061220dapr.jpg" height="556"></p>\n<h5><strong>Purge Public and Private Institutions of customs and symbols adverse to Wokeism.</strong></h5>\n<p>To facilitate the dominance of Woke doctrine, the heritage of previous widely adopted beliefs must be purged. Thus monuments of past American heroes must be destroyed, and doubt must be cast over the documents and writings of the founders of the American Republic. Rituals like the Pledge of Allegiance or prayers at public events must be replaced with pride rainbows or kneeling to BLM flags.</p>\n<p><img width="630" src="https://media.townhall.com/Townhall/Car/b/mle200614c20200613074358.jpg" height="440"></p>\n<h5><strong>Persecute and prosecute individuals whose speech and/or actions are contrary to Wokeism, or who are less than enthusiastic.</strong></h5>\n<p>To achieve Woke totalitarian dominance, dissenters must be reviled as heretics. And if unrepentent, they must be incarcerated or otherwise excommunicated by removing their reputations and employment. Any spokespersons for alternative opinions to Woke views of history and identity politics must be driven out of public awareness and discourse.</p>\n<p><img src="https://rclutz.files.wordpress.com/2021/08/sbr040921dapr20210409024516.jpg?w=1000&#038;h=750"></p>\n<h5><strong>Expand the administrative bureaucracy to extend social and economic control and to deepen dependency upon the state.</strong></h5>\n<p>By declaring states of emergency, a la Covid or Climate, Executive agencies will create more rules regulating enterprises, indeed all employees public and private, constraining personal choices to align with Woke doctrines. Expanding the regulatory authorities will greatly increase numbers on the public payroll, and shift financial power away from the private sector.</p>\n<p><img width="850" src="https://www.azquotes.com/picture-quotes/quote-in-a-country-where-the-sole-employer-is-the-state-opposition-means-death-by-slow-starvation-leon-trotsky-42-64-97.jpg" height="400"></p>\n<h5><strong>Strip the citizenry of firearms to prevent resistance to the force of governmental edicts.</strong></h5>\n<p>To complete the state\xe2\x80\x99s monopoly of coercion force over the populace, citizens\xe2\x80\x99 right to self-defense must be rendered mute by confiscating guns and ammunition.</p>\n<p><img width="858" src="https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=http%3A%2F%2Fs3.amazonaws.com%2Farmstrongeconomics-wp%2F2015%2F03%2FStalin-elections.jpg&amp;f=1&amp;nofb=1" height="407"></p>\n<h5><strong>Rig the election process so that the Woke party is always returned to power.</strong></h5>\n<p>In line with Marxist theory, Woke doctrine includes believing that only one political party is legitimate; that is the one representing the victims of oppression. All others are illegitimate, and cannot be allowed to form the government by means of a free and fair election. The illusion of voters choosing will be maintained, but political communication will be slanted to heavily favor the Woke appointed candidates. Also the collecting and counting of ballots will be coordinated to ensure the right outcome.</p>\n<p><img src="https://rclutz.files.wordpress.com/2018/10/52cb2-ramirezjusticeempathy.jpg?w=1000&#038;h=680"></p>\n<h5><strong>Neutralize Congress and Supreme Court as checks upon Executive authority.</strong></h5>\n<p>With the centralizing of governance in the Executive branch. Congress and the Courts must be relegated to advisory roles. Deliberation will occur in the agencies with Executive oversight, while congressional discussions will give the appearance of representation for the electors. The courts will limit themselves to reaffirming Woke doctrine against heresies arising from time to time.</p>\n<h5><strong>Postscript</strong></h5>\n<p>I have used satirical images to poke holes in this mistaken political movement, but this is a serious moment in the struggle for the soul and future of the American Republic.\xc2\xa0 For example, note these statements excerpted from a recent fundraising email from Ron DeSantis, addressing these same points in resolute language.</p>\n<p><em>Our country is currently facing a great threat. A new enemy has emerged from the shadows that seeks to destroy and intimidate their way to a transformed state, and country, that you and I would hardly recognize.</em></p>\n<p><em>This enemy is the radical vigilante woke mob that will steamroll anything and anyone in their way. Their blatant attacks on the American way of life are clear and intensifying: stifling dissent, public shaming, rampant violence, and a perverted version of history.</em></p>\n<p><em>A group that will, literally, tear down monuments and buildings but \xe2\x80\x94 perhaps in an even more sinister way \xe2\x80\x94 tear down the American spirit itself.</em></p>\n<p><em>To destroy America, they go after the family unit, parental rights, traditional moral values, the church, and fact-based education.</em></p>\n<p><em>Over the past few years, we\xe2\x80\x99ve watched horrified as this group has attempted to brainwash our children into thinking we live in an evil, racist, irredeemable country.</em></p>\n<p><em>With regard to Covid, we listened to them deny science and data to exert political theater all the while trampling over personal liberties enshrined in the Constitution.</em></p>\n<p><em>We saw them take to the streets for an entire summer like outlaws burning, looting, and destroying everything in sight while being told they were \xe2\x80\x9cmostly peaceful\xe2\x80\x9d and \xe2\x80\x9cpassionate.\xe2\x80\x9d</em></p>\n<p><em>We watched Big Tech moguls in Silicon Valley be the arbiters of truth \xe2\x80\x93 deciding who gets to speak and who gets silenced through the digital public square.</em></p>\n<p><em>We The People still have a say. We know the truth, you and I, about America and the country she is and can be. We must fight to defeat these false pretenses and predetermined narratives.</em></p>\n<p><em>I am choosing to counter this enemy with faith, with reason, and with freedom. As Governor of the Free State of Florida, I have chosen to lead with a vision that builds America up rather than tears it down.</em></p>\n<p><em>Together we can ensure that our children are raised to know they live in the greatest state in the nation, the greatest country in the world and that they have an opportunity to continue making them even greater.</em></p>\n<p><em>Am I embarrassed to speak for a less than perfect democracy? Not one bit. Find me a better one. Do I suppose there are societies which are free of sin? No, I don\xe2\x80\x99t. class="entry-title">Claim: Oil Markets Face a \xe2\x80\x9cDoomsday Scenario\xe2\x80\x9d Supply&nbsp;Shock</h1></a>\n\t\t</div><!-- .entry-header-column -->\n\t</div><!-- .entry-header-row -->\n</header><!-- .entry-header -->\n\t<div class="entry-content">\n\t<div>\n<div>\n<div>\n<p><strong>Essay by Eric Worrall</strong></p>\n<p>Think you have already seen high gasoline prices? <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://oilprice.com/Energy/Energy-General/Oil-Markets-Could-Face-A-Doomsday-Scenario-This-Week.html" target="_blank">OilPrice.com</a> claims chronic underinvestment, excessive taxation, and growing political turmoil in producer nations, could trigger a &#8220;Doomsday Scenario&#8221; as demand outstrips supply. </p>\n<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">\n<p><strong>Oil Markets Could Face A Doomsday Scenario This Week</strong></p>\n<p>By&#160;<a href="https://oilprice.com/contributors/Cyril-Widdershoven">Cyril Widdershoven</a>&#160;&#8211; Jun 28, 2022, 7:00 PM CDT</p>\n<p>Expect lots of oil price volatility in the coming months as markets finally discover just how much spare capacity OPEC members really have.</p>\n<p>Oil production outages in Libya and the continued impact of Russia&#8217;s invasion of Ukraine are going to push oil prices higher if new supply isn&#8217;t found.</p>\n<p>While some analysts are predicting oil demand destruction in the near future, there is little evidence to back up those claims.</p>\n<p>&#8230;</p>\n<p>This week, a possible doomsday scenario could emerge in oil markets, based not only on OPEC+ export strategies but also due to increased internal turmoil in Libya, Iraq, and Ecuador. Possible other political and economic turmoil is also brewing in other producers, while US shale is still not showing any signs of a substantial production increase in the coming months.&#160;</p>\n<p>Global oil markets have long believed that OPEC has enough spare production capacity to stabilize markets, with Saudi Arabia and the UAE just needing to open their taps. There is ,however, no real evidence to suggest that OPEC has increased production capacity in place in the short term. A research note by Commonwealth Bank commodities analyst Tobin Gorey already noted that OPEC&#8217;s two leaders are producing at near-term capacity limits. At the same time, UAE Minister of Energy Suhail Al Mazrouei put even more pressure on oil prices as he stated that the UAE is producing near-maximum capacity based on its quota of 3.168 million barrels per day (bpd) under the agreement with OPEC and its allies. That comment could still indicate that there is some spare capacity left in Abu Dhabi, but the remarks were made after French President Emmanuel Macron had stated to US president Biden during the G7 meeting that not only is the UAE producing at maximum production capacity, but also that Saudi Arabia only has another 150,000 bpd of spare capacity available.&#160;</p>\n<p>&#8230;</p>\n<p>Pressure will build in the coming days, as Al Mazrouei&#8217;s remarks seem to rebuke claims of a spare capacity shortage, but as always &#8220;where there is smoke, there is a fire&#8221;.&#160; A possible spare production capacity shortage, or non-availability at all, combined with an expected force majeure of Libya&#8217;s NOC in the Gulf of Sirte, and a suspension of Ecuador&#8217;s oil output (520,000 bpd) in the coming days due to anti-government protests, are likely to lead to an oil price spike.&#160;</p>\n<p>&#8230;</p>\n<p><cite>Read more: <a href="https://oilprice.com/Energy/Energy-General/Oil-Markets-Could-Face-A-Doomsday-Scenario-This-Week.html" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://oilprice.com/Energy/Energy-General/Oil-Markets-Could-Face-A-Doomsday-Scenario-This-Week.html</a></cite></p></blockquote>\n<p>If only there was a major oil pipeline project the Biden administration could approve, to provide Americans with a capacity buffer against what could be a nasty additional supply shock, on top of all the oil price pain people have already experienced. </p>\n<p>Thankfully the USA&#8217;s green energy transition federal government is on the case. If you find the next gasoline price hike utterly unaffordable, if you are struggling to pay the bills, you could <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://wattsupwiththat.com/2022/06/07/sen-debbie-stabenow-d-mi-has-her-ev-so-gasoline-prices-dont-matter/" target="_blank">follow Democrat advice, and borrow $50,000 to buy an EV</a>.
